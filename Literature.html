Some authors influential to the African American Public are:

Chinua Achebe: Nigerian novelist, poet, professor, and critic

Chimamanda ngozi adichie: Nigerian novelist, nonfiction writer and short story writer.

Toni Morrison: American novelist and editor

Richard Wright: American author of (sometimes controversial) novels, short stories, poems, and non-fiction

Zora Neale Hurston: American novelist, short story writer, folklorist, and anthropologist.
